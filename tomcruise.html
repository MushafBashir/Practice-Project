TOM CRUISE

Tom Cruise is a renowned American actor and producer, born on July 3, 1962, in Syracuse, New York. He rose to fame with iconic roles in films like "Top Gun," "Mission: Impossible," and "Jerry Maguire." Cruise is celebrated for his charismatic performances, intense action sequences, and status as one of Hollywood's biggest box office draws.
