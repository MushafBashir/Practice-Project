MARK TWAIN

Mark Twain, the literary luminary of the 19th century, navigated the currents of his era with a pen as sharp as his wit. His words, a reflection of both the Gilded Age's exuberance and its contradictions, continue to captivate minds and hearts, painting an enduring portrait of a nation in flux.
