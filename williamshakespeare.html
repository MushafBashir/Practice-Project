WILLIAM SHAKESPEARE

William Shakespeare (1564-1616) was an iconic English playwright, poet, and actor, widely regarded as one of the greatest writers in the English language. His works, encompassing plays like "Romeo and Juliet," "Hamlet," and "Macbeth," have left an indelible mark on literature and drama. Shakespeare's profound exploration of human emotions and his mastery of language continue to captivate audiences and influence artistic expression worldwide.
