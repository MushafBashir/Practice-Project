ALBERT EINSTEIN

Albert Einstein was one of the most brilliant and influential physicists of all time. He developed the theories of special and general relativity, which revolutionized our understanding of space, time, gravity, and energy. He also discovered the photoelectric effect, which showed that light has both wave and particle properties, and paved the way for quantum physics. He won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921 for his work on the photoelectric effect. He is widely admired for his scientific genius, his pacifist ideals, and his iconic hairstyle.
