MAULANA RUMI

Maulana Rumi, also known as Mevlana Jalaluddin Rumi, was a 13th-century Persian poet, jurist, Islamic scholar, theologian, and Sufi mystic. He is widely celebrated for his profound and timeless poetry, which continues to resonate with people from all walks of life. Rumi's work transcends cultural and religious boundaries, touching the hearts and souls of individuals across the globe. His verses explore themes of love, spirituality, and the quest for divine union.
