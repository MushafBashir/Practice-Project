ARISTOTLE

Aristotle, the revered ancient Greek philosopher, stands as one of history's towering intellectual figures. As a student of Plato and tutor to Alexander the Great, he laid the foundations of Western thought through his profound contributions to metaphysics, ethics, politics, and natural sciences. His insatiable curiosity and rigorous analytical prowess continue to inspire generations, making him an enduring beacon of wisdom and philosophical insight.
